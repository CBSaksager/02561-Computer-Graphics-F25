As can be seen above the minification filter has a big impact, and can lose a lot of detail if we use the Linear filter.
I chose to use nearest as the mountains texture still looks like mountains, while linear makes it look very blurry.
The coastline also becomes very muddy with the linear filtering.
With nearest it does look a little blocky, but these pictures are very zoomed in, and it can be hard to tell when the image is at normal zoom.
